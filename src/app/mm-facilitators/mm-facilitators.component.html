Mohita Singh

Occupational Health Safety Consultant

Mohita Singh is a Somatic & Expressive Movement Facilitator, dedicated to enriching the physical and emotional strength of people through Movement Therapy programs.

She also believes that with a good physical foundation, emotional and intellectual expression too become richer emotional and intellectual.
